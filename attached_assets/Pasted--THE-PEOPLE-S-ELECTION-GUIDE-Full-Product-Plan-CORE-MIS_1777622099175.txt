🗳️ THE PEOPLE'S ELECTION GUIDE — Full Product Plan

CORE MISSION
Nonpartisan. Transparent. Citizen-first.
No party logos. No political ads. No agenda. Just the process — explained clearly for every citizen.

THE 7 PAGES
PAGE 1 — HOME

Big bold headline: "Democracy is Yours. Learn How It Works."
Short mission statement (2 lines max)
4 quick-access tiles: Register → Learn → Vote → Your Rights
A live "Days to Next Election" countdown
Language switcher (Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, English etc.)


PAGE 2 — WHAT IS AN ELECTION?

Simple explainer: What elections are and WHY they matter
Types of elections: General, State, Local, By-elections, Panchayat
Key terms glossary (Constituency, Ballot, EVM, Candidate, Manifesto)
"Did You Know?" fact cards
Short animated infographic on how a vote travels from booth → result


PAGE 3 — ELECTION TIMELINE (Interactive)

A visual step-by-step journey from Announcement → Results
Steps: Election Announced → Voter List Published → Nomination Filed → Campaigning → Silence Period → Voting Day → Counting → Results
Each step is clickable — expands to explain what happens, who does what, and what the citizen should do at that stage
Color-coded phases (Registration phase, Campaign phase, Voting phase)


PAGE 4 — HOW TO VOTE (Citizen Step Guide)

Step 1: Am I eligible? (Age, citizenship checker)
Step 2: Am I on the voter list? (Link to official roll)
Step 3: How to register / update my details
Step 4: What to bring on voting day (Voter ID, Aadhar, alternatives)
Step 5: What happens inside the polling booth
Step 6: How to check if your vote was counted (VVPAT explanation)
Downloadable checklist for voting day


PAGE 5 — YOUR RIGHTS AS A VOTER

Right to vote without pressure or influence
Right to a secret ballot
Right to file a complaint if denied
Right to NOTA (None of the Above)
What is a Model Code of Conduct — and why it protects YOU
Who to call / where to report violations (helpline numbers)
"What if I was bribed?" section — clear, judgment-free guidance


PAGE 6 — ASK THE GUIDE (AI Chat Assistant)

Conversational AI assistant powered by Claude
Answers questions like:

"How do I register if I just turned 18?"
"What if my name isn't on the list?"
"Can I vote if I moved cities?"
"What is Form 6?"


Strictly nonpartisan — no party opinions, no political bias
Quick-reply suggestion chips for common questions
Disclaimer: "For official decisions, always verify with your local Election Commission"


PAGE 7 — QUIZ: HOW MUCH DO YOU KNOW?

8 quick questions on the election process
Multiple choice, instant feedback
Score card at the end with "Learn More" links to relevant pages
Shareable result: "I scored 7/8 on the People's Election Quiz!"
Replayable with shuffled questions
